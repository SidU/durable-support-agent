# Contoso Electronics Support Knowledge Base

## Return Policy

Contoso Electronics offers a 30-day return policy for all products purchased through our store. Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition. To initiate a return, contact our support team with your order number. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs for returns are covered by Contoso for defective items; otherwise, the customer is responsible for return shipping.

## Warranty Information

All Contoso Electronics products come with a standard 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications. Extended warranty plans (2-year and 3-year) are available for purchase within 30 days of the original order. To file a warranty claim, provide your order number and a description of the issue.

## Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days and is free for orders over $50. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available for $9.99. Overnight shipping is available for $19.99. All orders are shipped via trusted carriers with tracking numbers provided via email. International shipping is not currently available.

## Duplicate Charges

If you notice a duplicate charge on your account, please contact support immediately. Duplicate charges can occur due to payment processing errors and are typically resolved within 24 hours. We will investigate the charge and issue a refund for any confirmed duplicate. Please have your order number ready when contacting support.

## Product Troubleshooting — Wireless Mouse

If your Contoso Wireless Mouse is not connecting, try the following steps:
1. Ensure the mouse is charged (LED should be green when powered on).
2. Remove and re-insert the USB receiver.
3. Press the reset button on the bottom of the mouse for 3 seconds.
4. Try a different USB port.
5. If the issue persists, contact support for a replacement under warranty.

## Product Troubleshooting — Mechanical Keyboard

If keys on your Contoso Mechanical Keyboard are not responding:
1. Unplug and reconnect the USB cable.
2. Check that no debris is lodged under the keycaps.
3. Test with a different computer to rule out a software issue.
4. Update your keyboard firmware via the Contoso Devices app.
5. If the issue persists, contact support for warranty service.

## Order Status

You can check your order status at any time by providing your order number to our support team. Order statuses include: processing, shipped, delivered, and cancelled. Tracking information is sent via email once your order ships.

## Account and Membership Tiers

Contoso Electronics offers three membership tiers: Bronze, Silver, and Gold. Bronze members receive standard support. Silver members get priority support and free express shipping. Gold members receive priority support, free overnight shipping, and early access to new products. Tier status is based on annual purchase history.
